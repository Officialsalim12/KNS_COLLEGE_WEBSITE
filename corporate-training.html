Corporate Training Solutions

Since 2000, KNS College has been at the forefront of providing customized and industry-relevant skill-building workshops and training to small, medium, and large organizations across Sierra Leone. As the "Digital Skills Champion 2025" recognized by the Ministry of Communication, Technology & Innovation (MoCTI), we are committed to empowering your workforce with globally recognized certifications and practical skills.

Our corporate training solutions are designed to bridge the gap between your organization's current capabilities and the skills needed to thrive in today's digital economy. Every training program integrates industry-recognized certifications from leading partners like Cisco, Microsoft, PMI, (ISC)², Certiport, and more, ensuring your employees gain credentials that are valued worldwide.

Why Choose KNS College for Corporate Training?

  • Customized Training Programmes: Tailored to your organization's specific needs and industry requirements
  • Flexible Delivery Options: Training can be conducted at your company site, our campus at 18 Dundas Street, Freetown, or online
  • Flexible Scheduling: Choose from weekdays, evenings, or weekends to minimize disruption to your operations
  • Industry-Certified Instructors: Learn from highly qualified trainers with real-world industry experience and certifications
  • Globally Recognized Certifications: Employees earn industry credentials from Cisco, Microsoft, PMI, (ISC)², Certiport, and more
  • Authorized Testing Center: Convenient on-site certification exams at our Pearson VUE and Certiport Authorized Testing Centers
  • Competitive Pricing: We offer competitive rates and can work with your budget to create a training solution that fits
  • Proven Track Record: Over 20 years of experience training professionals across Sierra Leone
