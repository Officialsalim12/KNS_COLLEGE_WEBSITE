How to Submit

Important: We do not accept online form submissions. All applications must be submitted via email.
  1. Complete the downloaded application form with all required information.
  2. Gather all required supporting documents (transcripts, letters of recommendation, etc.).
  3. Scan or take clear photos of all documents.
  4. Compose an email to admissions@kns.edu.sl
  5. Use the following subject line format:
    Application: [Scholarship Name] - [Your Full Name]

    Example: Application: Merit Scholarship - John Doe

  6. Attach all completed documents and supporting materials to the email.
  7. Send the email before the deadline (see below).
