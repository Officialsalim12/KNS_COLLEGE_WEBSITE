Scholarship Guide

Scholarship Overview

The KNS College Scholarship Programme helps students who are hardworking and want to study diploma courses at KNS College. This programme gives financial support, internationally recognized certifications, and opportunities for professional growth. It helps students excel in their studies and build strong career pathways.

Scholarship Programme Year: 2026
Application Deadline: 19 January 2025

Available Diploma Programmes (2-Year Duration)

Choose one of these diploma programmes:

  • Cybersecurity
  • Telecommunications
  • Project Management
  • Entrepreneurship & Small Business
  • Applied Computerized Accounting (QuickBooks)
  • IT with Business Management
  • Computing & Networking

Scholarship Categories

Applicants can apply for one of these options:

  • Full Scholarship – Covers 100% of tuition fees
  • Partial Scholarship – Minimum 60% tuition fee discount

Eligibility Criteria

To be considered for the scholarship, applicants must:

  • Have at least one recognized academic qualification (WASSCE, Certificate, Diploma, or similar)
  • Show strong interest and clear career plans
  • Be ready and committed to complete the full course
  • Submit a complete application with all required documents before the deadline

Application Process

  1. Step 1: Personal Information
    Provide your full name (surname, first name, and other names), gender, date of birth, nationality, and National ID or Passport number.
  2. Step 2: Contact Information
    Provide a valid residential address, city/town, telephone number(s), and email address.
  3. Step 3: Academic Background
    State your highest academic qualification and include the name of school/institution, year of completion, and number of credits (if available).
  4. Step 4: Programme Selection
    Choose one preferred diploma programme from the approved list above.
  5. Step 5: Scholarship Type Selection
    Indicate whether you are applying for a Full Scholarship or a Partial Scholarship (minimum 60% tuition discount). Also mention if you have applied for any scholarship before.
  6. Step 6: Personal Statement (Mandatory)
    Write a personal statement of not less than 300 words explaining:
    • Why you are applying for the KNS College Scholarship
    • How the selected programme will affect your career and future goals
    • Why you should be considered for the scholarship
  7. Step 7: Supporting Documents Submission
    Prepare and include all required supporting documents listed below.
  8. Step 8: Declaration
    Sign and date a declaration confirming that all information provided is true, accurate, and complete. Giving false information can result in disqualification.

Required Supporting Documents

Mandatory Documents

  • Academic Certificate(s) or Result Slip
  • Valid National ID or Passport
  • Passport-size Photograph
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)

Optional (Advantageous)

  • Recommendation Letter

Submission Details

Completed applications must be submitted on or before 19 January 2025 through any of these methods:

  • Email: admissions@kns.edu.sl
  • Address: 18 Dundas Street, Freetown

Important Notes

  • Late applications will not be considered.
  • Incomplete applications may be disqualified.
  • Only shortlisted candidates will be contacted.
  • Successful applicants will be officially notified by KNS College.

How to Submit

Application Options: You can apply using either of the two methods below. Choose the option that is most convenient for you.

Option 1: Download and Email Application

  1. Download the application form using the "Download Application Form" button in the Quick Actions sidebar.
  2. Complete the application form with all required information.
  3. Gather all required supporting documents (transcripts, letters of recommendation, etc.).
  4. Scan or take clear photos of the completed application form and all supporting documents.
  5. Compose an email to admissions@kns.edu.sl
  6. Use the following subject line format:
    Application: [Scholarship Name] - [Your Full Name]

    Example: Application: Merit Scholarship - Abdul Saidu Kamara

  7. Attach the completed application form and all supporting documents to the email.
  8. Send the email before the deadline (see below).

Option 2: Online Google Form Application

  1. Fill Application on Google Form and complete the online application form with all required information.
  2. Submit the online form through Google Forms.
  3. Important: After submitting the online form, you must bring all your supporting documents (transcripts, letters of recommendation, etc.) to the office at 18 Dundas Street, Freetown.
  4. Ensure you submit your supporting documents before the application deadline (see below).
