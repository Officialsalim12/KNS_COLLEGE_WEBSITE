Diploma in Enterprise and Small Business

Launch Your Venture. Build Your Business. Become Your Own Boss.

Industry Certifications Included

Entrepreneurship and Small Business (ESB)
Certiport
Credly Digital Badge

Programme Summary

Designed for the next generation of Sierra Leonean innovators and job creators, this diploma provides the essential skills to start, manage, and grow a small business. Whether you have a new idea or want to formalize an existing hustle, this programme covers everything from business planning and marketing to financial management.

A Diploma in Enterprise and Small Business can advance your career, increase your earnings, or help you find a job you enjoy. At KNS College, your existing knowledge can accelerate your progress through the program. With our online and hybrid courses, you have complete flexibility there's no need to log in at specific times. You control your learning schedule. Plus, you'll graduate with both a diploma and leading industry certifications, all included in your tuition. Prepare for a rewarding and high-paying career by earning your diploma in enterprise and small business from KNS College.

Top Information Technology Certifications at No Extra Cost

Credentials matter in the IT field and showcasing both your skills and achievements is essential. At KNS College, we make it easy for you to earn a Diploma alongside valuable industry certifications. Every one of our IT Programmes includes preparation for top certification exams at no additional cost, since exam fees are covered by your tuition. This means you'll graduate with more than just a diploma; you'll also have industry-recognized certifications to enhance your resume, increase your earning potential, and help you stand out to employers even before you finish your program.

Entrepreneurship and Small Business (ESB) Certification

This programme is integrated with the Entrepreneurship and Small Business (ESB) certification from Certiport, giving you a globally recognized credential that proves your business acumen. Your fees include the ESB exam voucher and a Credly digital badge, validating your expertise in starting, managing, and growing small businesses.

Why a Diploma and Certifications?

At KNS College, your success is our priority. That's why our Diploma programmes are designed to include leading industry IT certifications, delivered online or in class for your convenience. Earning these certifications alongside your diploma not only strengthens your technical expertise but also increases your marketability to employers. With advanced credentials and specialized skill sets, you'll stand out as a highly qualified candidate in the competitive IT job market.

Pursuing IT certifications can often be costly and require a significant investment of time. At KNS College, we aim to make this process more accessible and streamlined for our students. By integrating select industry certification fees into your tuition and embedding certification preparation directly into our curriculum, you will graduate from KNS College with both your diploma and valuable, in-demand certifications. This combination not only broadens your skill set but also opens the door to greater career opportunities in the field of information technology.

Programme Details

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
Study Time: 3-5 hours per course per week
Language: English
Delivery Mode: Online / Hybrid
Credential: Diploma + ESB (Certiport) Certification
Intake Periods: January, May, September
Assessment: Business Plan Capstone Project (40%), Exams (30%), Coursework (30%)

Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this diploma, you will be able to:

  • Identify and evaluate business opportunities in the local market.
  • Develop a comprehensive and viable business plan.
  • Understand the legal and regulatory requirements for starting a business in Sierra Leone.
  • Create and implement effective marketing and sales strategies.
  • Manage small business finances, including bookkeeping, budgeting, and sourcing capital.
  • Leverage digital tools to enhance business operations and reach more customers.

Programme Structure

This two-year programme follows a semester structure (2 per year, 15 weeks each). The expected study time is 3-5 hours per course per week. Assessment is heavily project-based: Business Plan Capstone Project (40%), Exams (30%), and Coursework (30%).

Year 1 Modules

Compulsory Modules

  • Business Law & Ethics for SMEs
  • Marketing Fundamentals
  • Financial Accounting for Small Business
  • Business Communication & Pitching
  • Digital Literacy for Entrepreneurs

Year 2 Modules

Compulsory Modules

  • Small Business Management & Operations
  • Digital Marketing & E-commerce
  • Sales & Customer Relationship Management
  • Human Resource Basics for SMEs
  • Industry Module: ESB Certification Prep
  • Capstone Project: Business Plan Development & Pitch
