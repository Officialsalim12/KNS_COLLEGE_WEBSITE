Start Your Journey with KNS College

Entry Requirements

We believe in providing access to quality education for all passionate individuals. Our entry requirements are designed to welcome a diverse range of applicants.

Diploma Programmes

  • A minimum of three (3) credits in the West African Senior School Certificate Examination (WASSCE). OR
  • General Certificate of Education (GCE)

Certificate Courses

  • A minimum of two (2) credits in WASSCE. OR
  • Demonstrate strong enthusiasm and aptitude for technology/Business (for credits)

Rolling Enrollment

We offer flexible start dates to fit your life. You can enroll and begin your studies for Diploma Programmes in January, May, or September.

How to Register

Applying to KNS College is simple and straightforward. Follow these four easy steps to begin your journey.

1

Choose Your Programme

Explore our Diploma and Certificate courses and select the one that aligns with your career goals.

2

Apply Online

Visit our website at www.kns.edu.sl/apply to fill out the online application form. It only takes about 10 minutes.

3

Submit Documents

Upload scanned copies of your WASSCE result slip and a valid form of identification (National ID, Passport).

4

Admission & Payment

Our admissions team will review your application and send you an offer letter. Follow the instructions to pay your fees and secure your spot.
