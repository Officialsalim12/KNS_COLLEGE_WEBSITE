PMI offers many certifications that allow project management professionals to demonstrate that they are well versed in project management methodologies, prepared to work with specific frameworks, and can handle complex projects in various industries. The PMI Project Management Ready certification is optimally positioned as a first step in a career or interest in project management. It is especially valuable if you are new to the field, still studying, or early in your career. Earning PMI certifications can boost your résumé and allow you to stand out in a competitive job market.

KNS College PMI Certifications

KNS College is pleased to offer the following PMI certification and exam to IT students at no extra cost beyond tuition:

1

PMI Project Management Ready

The PMI Project Management Ready certification introduces learners to the fundamental principles, methodologies, and professional language of modern project management, making it an ideal starting point for individuals with little or no prior experience in the field. By giving you recognized credentials and shared professional vocabulary (methodologies, frameworks, foundational practices), it helps you stand out when applying for entry-level project-management roles or internships.

This certification introduces students to both predictive (Waterfall) project management and adaptive (Agile) approaches, ensuring they understand how contemporary organizations plan, execute, monitor, and deliver successful outcomes. If you aim for long-term career development in project management — especially handling larger or riskier projects — this certification can serve as a foundation before pursuing more advanced and widely recognized credentials from PMI.

You must pass an exam to earn the certification. The PMI Project Management Ready exam lasts about one to two hours. Your KNS College IT coursework will prepare you to take the exam. Your Program Mentor or Course Instructor may provide guidance on how to prepare for and schedule your exam.

The certification demonstrates your commitment to excellence and provides opportunities to work across diverse sectors, geographies, and project management methodologies.

(ISC)²

View IT Diplomas

(ISC)², formally known as the International Information System Security Certification Consortium, is the world's leading nonprofit organization dedicated to advancing the cybersecurity profession. Established in 1989, (ISC)² develops globally recognized cybersecurity standards, certifications, and professional development resources that help individuals and organizations build and maintain strong security capabilities. (ISC)² is best known for administering some of the most respected cybersecurity certifications in the world. Earning (ISC)² certifications can boost your résumé and allow you to stand out in a competitive job market.

KNS College (ISC)² Certifications

KNS College is pleased to offer the following (ISC)² certification and exam to IT students at no extra cost beyond tuition:

1

Certified in Cybersecurity (CC)

Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) is an entry-level cybersecurity certification offered by (ISC)², the world's leading cybersecurity credentialing body. It is designed for individuals with little or no IT experience who want to start a career in cybersecurity. The CC certification validates foundational knowledge and proves that a learner understands the essential principles needed to work in a modern cybersecurity environment.

The CC certification covers five fundamental domains: Security Principles, Business Continuity/Disaster Recovery & Incident Response Concepts, Access Controls Concepts, Network Security, and Security Operations. The exam is designed to test baseline knowledge required for junior-level cyber roles, such as security analyst trainee, SOC support staff, IT support with security focus, or cybersecurity intern.

(ISC)² Certified in Cybersecurity Exams

You must pass an exam to earn the certification. The (ISC)² Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) exam lasts about two hours. Your KNS College IT coursework will prepare you to take the exam. Your Program Mentor or Course Instructor may provide guidance on how to prepare for and schedule your exam.

(ISC)² Certified in Cybersecurity Certifications

(ISC)² Certified in Cybersecurity (CC) exam vouchers are valued at $199 USD. However, KNS College gives IT students vouchers to take the certification exam as part of their tuition. The certification demonstrates your commitment to cybersecurity excellence and provides opportunities to work across diverse sectors and geographies. Because the certification has no prerequisites, it is highly accessible to students, career changers, and individuals seeking to enter the cybersecurity workforce.

KNS College Cisco Certifications

KNS College is pleased to offer the following Cisco certification and exam to IT students at no extra cost beyond tuition:

1

Cisco Certified Support Technician (CCST)

The Cisco Certified Support Technician (CCST) certification is Cisco's official entry-level credential designed to help learners build a strong foundation in IT, networking, and cybersecurity. CCST is specifically created for students and beginners who are starting their journey in the technology field and want to develop hands-on, job-ready skills that meet global industry standards.

Cisco offers two CCST certification pathways: CCST Networking – focused on essential networking concepts, IP addressing, troubleshooting, routing, Wi-Fi technologies, and supporting small to medium network environments; and CCST Cybersecurity – focused on cybersecurity fundamentals, common threats and vulnerabilities, access control, security monitoring, and basic incident response. Both CCST certifications provide practical, foundational knowledge that prepares students for real-world IT support, network operations, and cybersecurity roles.

Cisco CCST Exams

You must pass an exam to earn the certification. Cisco offers two CCST certification exams, each delivered through the Certiport testing platform at KNS College. The CCST Networking exam measures foundational knowledge in networking concepts, IP addressing, routing, switching, Wi-Fi and wired technologies, troubleshooting connectivity issues, and basic security principles. The CCST Cybersecurity exam validates introductory cybersecurity knowledge including security fundamentals, threats and vulnerabilities, access control, network protection, and incident response basics. Both exams last about one to two hours and can be taken in person at KNS College Exam center.

Cisco CCST Certifications

Cisco CCST exam vouchers are valued at $125 USD. However, KNS College gives IT students vouchers to take the certification exam as part of their tuition. Preparation typically takes three to four months and is integrated into the diploma curriculum. Upon passing the exam, candidates earn an industry-recognized Cisco Certified Support Technician certification that demonstrates practical competence in troubleshooting and IT support, readiness for entry-level networking or cybersecurity roles, and a strong foundation for more advanced certifications.

Microsoft offers many certifications that allow developers and IT professionals to demonstrate that they are well versed in Microsoft software, prepared to work with specific platforms, and can handle heavy workloads in the field of cloud computing. In addition to training in Microsoft 365 and Azure, specific certifications are tailored to developers, data engineers, and DevOps professionals. Earning Microsoft certifications can boost your résumé and allow you to stand out in a competitive job market.

KNS College Microsoft Certifications

There are many different groupings of Microsoft certifications aligned with job roles, including developer, administrator, solution architect, data engineer, data scientist, AI engineer, DevOps engineer, security engineer, and functional consultant. There are also varying levels of certification, starting with fundamentals, and progressing to associate and expert.

KNS College is pleased to offer the following Microsoft certifications and exams to IT students at no extra cost beyond tuition:

1

Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS)

The Microsoft Office Specialist (MOS) certification will help you master Microsoft Office applications, teaching you core skills in Word, Excel, PowerPoint, and Outlook. Once you complete this certification, you should be able to demonstrate proficiency in essential business software applications. This certification validates your expertise with Microsoft Office technologies and is recognized globally by employers.

2

Microsoft Azure Fundamentals (AZ-900)

The Azure Fundamentals certification will help you master the basics of Microsoft Azure, teaching you cloud concepts and core Azure services and features. Once you complete this certification, you should be able to explain security and general network features. You'll also become familiar with governance, identity, privacy, and compliance.

3

Microsoft 365 Fundamentals (MS-900)

The Microsoft 365 Fundamentals certification validates foundational knowledge of Microsoft 365 and its services. This certification helps you understand cloud concepts, Microsoft 365 pricing and support, and the security, compliance, privacy, and trust principles of Microsoft 365.

4

Microsoft Security, Compliance, and Identity Fundamentals (SC-900)

The Microsoft Security, Compliance, and Identity Fundamentals certification covers security and compliance concepts related to Microsoft 365 and Azure. This certification helps you understand security concepts, identity and access management, and compliance capabilities across Microsoft solutions.

Microsoft Exams

You must pass exams to earn certifications. Microsoft exams last about one to two hours, depending on the level of exam. Your KNS College IT coursework will prepare you to take the exams. Your Program Mentor or Course Instructor may provide guidance on how to prepare for and schedule your exams.

Microsoft Certifications

Microsoft certifications can cost around $83 to $165 USD. However, KNS College gives IT students vouchers to take the certification exams as part of their tuition. Fundamentals certifications do not expire. Role-based and specialty certifications expire after one year, and those that have been available for over six months have a renewal option. To renew a specialty certification, you will not need to retake a full exam. Rather, you can take a free online renewal assessment.
