Programme Summary

This diploma is the cornerstone for any career in Information Technology, providing essential skills in computer hardware, software, and network administration. It is perfect for individuals who want to become the go-to experts for installing, managing, and troubleshooting IT systems.

A Diploma in Computing and Networking can advance your career, increase your earnings, or help you find a job you enjoy. At KNS College, your existing knowledge can accelerate your progress through the program. With our online and hybrid courses, you have complete flexibility there's no need to log in at specific times. You control your learning schedule. Plus, you'll graduate with both a diploma and leading industry certifications, all included in your tuition. Prepare for a rewarding and high-paying career by earning your diploma in computing and networking from KNS College.

Top Information Technology Certifications at No Extra Cost

Credentials matter in the IT field and showcasing both your skills and achievements is essential. At KNS College, we make it easy for you to earn a Diploma alongside valuable industry certifications. Every one of our IT Programmes includes preparation for top certification exams at no additional cost, since exam fees are covered by your tuition. This means you'll graduate with more than just a diploma; you'll also have industry-recognized certifications to enhance your resume, increase your earning potential, and help you stand out to employers even before you finish your program.

Cisco Certified Support Technician (CCST) Networking Certification

The curriculum is deeply practical, preparing you for the Cisco Certified Support Technician (CCST) Networking certification. This globally recognized credential validates your ability to manage and secure enterprise-grade networks. Your fees include the CCST exam voucher and a verifiable Credly digital badge to showcase your skills.

Why a Diploma and Certifications?

At KNS College, your success is our priority. That's why our Diploma programmes are designed to include leading industry IT certifications, delivered online or in class for your convenience. Earning these certifications alongside your diploma not only strengthens your technical expertise but also increases your marketability to employers. With advanced credentials and specialized skill sets, you'll stand out as a highly qualified candidate in the competitive IT job market.

Pursuing IT certifications can often be costly and require a significant investment of time. At KNS College, we aim to make this process more accessible and streamlined for our students. By integrating select industry certification fees into your tuition and embedding certification preparation directly into our curriculum, you will graduate from KNS College with both your diploma and valuable, in-demand certifications. This combination not only broadens your skill set but also opens the door to greater career opportunities in the field of information technology.

Programme Details

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
Study Time: 3-5 hours per course per week
Language: English
Delivery Mode: Online / Hybrid
Credential: Diploma + Cisco CCST Networking Certification
Intake Periods: January, May, September
Assessment: Exams (50%), Lab/Practical Projects (30%), Coursework (20%)

Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this diploma, you will be able to:

  • Install, configure, and troubleshoot computer hardware, software, and operating systems.
  • Design, implement, and manage small to medium-sized office networks.
  • Understand and apply TCP/IP networking protocols and services.
  • Provide effective IT support and resolve common user issues.
  • Implement basic network security measures to protect against common threats.
  • Administer server operating systems and manage user accounts and permissions.

Programme Structure

This two-year programme follows a semester structure (2 per year, 15 weeks each). The expected study time is 3-5 hours per course per week. Assessment is a blend of Exams (50%), Practical Lab Assignments (30%), and Coursework (20%).
