Programme Summary

This diploma is designed to meet the growing demand for accounting professionals who are proficient in modern financial software. It provides a strong foundation in accounting principles combined with intensive, hands-on training in Intuit QuickBooks, the leading accounting software for small and medium-sized enterprises (SMEs).

A Diploma in Applied Computerised Accounting can advance your career, increase your earnings, or help you find a job you enjoy. At KNS College, your existing knowledge can accelerate your progress through the program. With our online and hybrid courses, you have complete flexibility there's no need to log in at specific times. You control your learning schedule. Plus, you'll graduate with both a diploma and leading industry certifications, all included in your tuition. Prepare for a rewarding and high-paying career by earning your diploma in accounting from KNS College.

Top Information Technology Certifications at No Extra Cost

Programme Details

Duration: 2 Years (4 Semesters)
Study Time: 3-5 hours per course per week
Language: English
Delivery Mode: Online / Hybrid
Credential: Diploma + Intuit Certified Bookkeeping Professional
Intake Periods: January, May, September
Assessment: Exams (50%), Practical QuickBooks Projects (30%), Coursework (20%)

Learning Outcomes

Upon successful completion of this diploma, you will be able to:

  • Apply fundamental accounting and bookkeeping principles to business transactions.
  • Set up and manage a company's financial records using Intuit QuickBooks.
  • Process accounts payable, accounts receivable, and payroll accurately.
  • Prepare key financial statements, including the Balance Sheet and Income Statement.
  • Reconcile bank statements and manage cash flow effectively.
  • Understand the basics of taxation and compliance for SMEs in Sierra Leone.

Programme Structure

This two-year programme follows a semester structure (2 per year, 15 weeks each). The expected study time is 3-5 hours per course per week. Assessment is a blend of Exams (50%), Practical QuickBooks Projects (30%), and Coursework (20%).
